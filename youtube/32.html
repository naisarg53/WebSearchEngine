Our impact

People all over the world have used YouTube to follow their passions, connect with others, and develop economic opportunities for themselves and their communities. Explore the stories behind just a few of YouTube’s creative entrepreneurs here.

  • YouTube Impact
  • YouTube Impact Stories

Every day, YouTube helps people learn new skills, start businesses, create jobs, and enrich their lives. A study by Oxford Economics found that YouTube's creative ecosystem contributed approximately $923 million CAD to the Canadian GDP, equivalent to 34,100 Full Time Equivalent (FTE) jobs in Canada in 2020.
