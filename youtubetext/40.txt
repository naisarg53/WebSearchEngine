YouTube’s Culture & Trends site is intended as a public repository for useful YouTube data, explanations of current trends, and accurate statistics that help you better understand the next generation of creators and artists. What types of content should I expect to see? YouTube data and statistics — Verified information about creators, artists, videos, and trends. YouTube Records — Official lists of record-holding videos and channels across our most-asked-about metrics. Explainers and spotlights — Stories that connect the dots between data points and larger trends. Deep dives — YouTube data storytelling that brings the numbers to life through visualizations and context. How do I cite data and stories from this site? Do I have permission to use YouTube Culture & Trends content? All data and information on the YouTube Culture & Trends site is official and can be cited without further permission required.
